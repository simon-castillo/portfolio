Cleaning up imported text

Project

An internal reporting platform

Brief

Design a way for users to sanitise reports that have been imported automatically from another reporting system.

In user research sessions, we noticed that people’s existing method for cleaning up imported text was to paste the content into Word, edit, and paste back into the platform.

We wanted to let them sanitise the report more efficiently, in one screen.

So, at the end of the import flow, when the user lands on the editable report, I added a sidebar that maintains the original data - uneditable - for reference. The user can open this, compare directly to their new report and add or remove data as needed.

Statuses and notifications

Project

An internal reporting platform

Brief

To let users know the status of their reports, confirm when their actions are successful, and notify them about important system changes.

As statuses and notifications are a common design challenge, I began with secondary research, focusing on common layouts, transitions and best practice.

It was important that all of the alerts were highly useful and unobtrusive, otherwise users might learn to ignore them, so the solution used as little ‘design’ as possible. Metadata was removed, background colour was chosen to be noticeable but not distracting and language was kept concise.
